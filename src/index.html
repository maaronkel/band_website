The Strokes are an American rock band from Manhattan, New York. Formed in 1998.

They have been recognized as one of the most prominent bands of the garage rock and post-punk revivals,
aiding in the resurgence of indie rock in New York City.

Lead Singer Julian Casablancas has cited Lou Reed of The Velvet Underground as a major influence on his lyrics and singing style.
